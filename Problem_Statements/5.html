Companywise Lowest Employee

Your solution is being evaluated.
Click here after 1 minute to refresh this page.

In the previous section, you figured out which employees belong to which companies by following the employee-manager relationships. In this problem, for similar input, for each company, you have to figure out the name of the employee who is farthest away from the CEO of that company - in terms of the company hierarchy.

For example, if input.txt contained:

Vineel Phatak, 520, NOBODY
Ajay Joshi, 250, Vineel Phatak
Abhishek Chauhan, 120, Ajay Joshi
Jayesh Godse, 500, NOBODY
Vijaya Mundada, 60, Abhishek Chauhan
Shital Tuteja, 45, Jayesh Godse
Rajan Gawli, 700, Vineel Phatak
Zeba Khan, 300, Jayesh Godse
Chaitali Sood, 100, Zeba Khan
Sheila Rodrigues, 35, Vineel Phatak

Then, for Vineel Phatak's company, Vijaya Mundada is the employee who is farthest away from the CEO because Vijaya's manager is Abhishek Chauhan, Abhishek's Manager is Ajay Joshi, and Ajay's manager is Vineel - thus Vijaya is 3 levels away from Vineel. Similarly, Chaitali Sood is the farthest employee in Jayesh Godse's company because she is 2 levels away.

Thus, when your program is run, output.txt should contain:

Vineel Phatak: Vijaya Mundada
Jayesh Godse: Chaitali Sood

Note: don't worry if the order of the lines in your output don't exactly match the order here, but the name and the number in each line must match perfectly. Also, make sure that each line consists of just one name followed by a colon, then one blank space and then another name.
